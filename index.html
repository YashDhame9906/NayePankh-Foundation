Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government registered NGO and 12A & 80G certified organization founded during the COVID-19 pandemic by passionate students who wanted to support communities in difficult times.

What started as a small initiative has now grown into a youth-driven movement with volunteers across multiple cities, impacting thousands of lives through education, hunger relief, healthcare awareness, and social welfare.

Our Journey

Small Steps That Created A Big Impact

2020

COVID Relief Initiative

A group of passionate students started helping needy people by providing food, resources, and support during the difficult COVID-19 period.

2021

Official Foundation Launch

On 28 March 2021, NayePankh Foundation officially began its journey as a registered NGO committed to social welfare.

Today

Growing Across Communities

Today, NayePankh is a youth-driven organization working across multiple cities through education, hunger relief, hygiene awareness, and community programs.

Our Initiatives

Education For Every Child

Hunger Relief

Organizing food distribution drives and helping families and stray animals with basic needs.

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

Spreading awareness about health and hygiene and providing sanitary products to women in need.

Providing clothes and essential items to poor and homeless families to support their daily lives.

Community Welfare

Taking small but meaningful steps every day to solve challenges faced by underprivileged communities.
